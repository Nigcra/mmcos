# Seasons

On the official server, the seasons changed every 2 months and lasted for 45 days.

For example:

| N  | Date       |
|----|------------|
|  8 | 01.01.2018 |
|  9 | 01.03.2018 |
| 10 | 01.05.2018 |
| 11 | 01.07.2018 |
| 12 | 01.09.2018 |
| 13 | 01.11.2018 |

There should be such a rotation on the community server:

| N  | Start | End    |
|----|-------|--------|
| +1 | Jan 1 | Feb 15 |
| +2 | Mar 1 | Apr 15 |
| +3 | May 1 | Jun 15 |
| +4 | Jul 1 | Aug 15 |
| +5 | Sep 1 | Oct 16 |
| +6 | Nov 1 | Dec 16 |
